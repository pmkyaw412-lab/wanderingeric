Kakebo (家計簿) is my attempt to build the budgeting tool I actually keep using: envelope-style buckets, a five-second logging flow, and a monthly reset that forgives you instead of judging you.

Design goals: no accounts, no sync anxiety, and a home screen that answers one question — *how much wander money is left this month?*

Still in progress; a public demo will land here when it's ready.
